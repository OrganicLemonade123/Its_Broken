01 — Core Concept
WHAT IS IT?
🔍

Diagnose It

Users snap a photo or describe the problem in plain language. The AI identifies what's wrong — a cracked PVC joint, a worn brake pad, a loose outlet box — and classifies the repair type and difficulty level immediately.

🛠️

Fix It

A live, step-by-step guided repair flow walks the user through each action. Steps adapt dynamically — if the user hits a snag, the AI re-routes and offers alternatives based on what tools and parts they actually have on hand.

📚

Learn It

After every repair, users see a short "why it broke" explanation and preventive tips. Over time the app builds a personal repair history and skill profile — rewarding growth and building real mechanical confidence.

🌐

Share It

Community-sourced repair notes and ratings for specific make/model combinations. Users can post their own fix photos and tips — turning individual repairs into a collective knowledge base everyone benefits from.

03 — User Experience Flow
HOW IT WORKS
1

Report the Problem

User taps "Something's broken" and describes it — typed, spoken, or photographed. No jargon required. "My sink won't stop dripping" is plenty.

AI Action
NLP + vision model classifies the problem, infers severity, and matches to repair playbook library.
2

Confirm Diagnosis

App presents the diagnosis card — what's broken, why, and difficulty rating. User confirms or corrects, training the model over time.

AI Action
Retrieval-augmented generation pulls repair guides for the specific appliance make/model if available.
3

Gather Tools & Parts

The app generates a precise tool and parts checklist, cross-referenced with the user's inventory. Parts can be ordered directly from the screen.

AI Action
Parts search queries retailer APIs to surface current pricing and availability at nearby stores.
4

Follow Guided Repair

Step-by-step walkthrough with photos, annotations, and video clips where needed. Each step waits for user confirmation before advancing.

AI Action
Conversational AI answers mid-repair questions and dynamically re-routes if the user encounters unexpected conditions.
5

Verify the Fix

User photographs the repaired area. The AI confirms the repair looks correct, or flags anything that needs re-checking before closing out.

AI Action
Vision model inspects completion photo against known "good state" reference for that repair type.
6

Log & Learn

Fix is saved to the repair log with cost savings calculated. User earns skill points and receives preventive maintenance tips to avoid recurrence.

AI Action
Personalizes future recommendations and proactively schedules preventive check reminders based on repair history.
